Hypothesis Testing: Tenure vs Churn

Why

-- To test whether the average tenure of churned customers is significantly different from that of retained customers.
-- This helps confirm if shorter tenure is statistically linked to higher churn.

In[36]:
churned = df[df['Churn']=="Yes"]['tenure']
retained = df[df['Churn']=="No"]['tenure']

t_stat, p_value = stats.ttest_ind(churned, retained)
print("T-statistics:", t_stat)
print("P-value:", p_value)

T-statistics: -31.57955051135377
P-value: 7.99905796059022e-205

Hypothesis Testing: Tenure vs Churn

Hypotheses

  • H (Null Hypothesis): Average tenure of churned and retained customers is the same.
  • H (Alternative Hypothesis): Average tenure of churned customers is different (shorter) than retained customers.

Test Result

  • T-statistic: Negative value churned customers have lower tenure.
  • P-value: Very small ( 0.00).

Conclusion

Reject H.
There is a statistically significant difference: churned customers have shorter tenure compared to retained ones.

Key Insights & Recommendations

Insights

  • Overall churn rate is ~26.5% significant business risk.
  • Monthtomonth contracts have the highest churn (43%), while twoyear contracts have the lowest (3%).
  • Customers with Fiber optic internet churn the most (42%), DSL churn is moderate (19%), and those with no internet service churn the least (%).
  • Electronic check users churn heavily (45%), while automatic payments (bank transfer/credit card) reduce churn (1517%).
  • Higher monthly charges are linked to higher churn (74 vs 61 average).
  • Short tenure customers are most likely to churn; statistical tests confirm tenure is significantly lower for churned customers.

Recommendations

  • Target earlystage customers (tenure < 12 months) with onboarding programs, loyalty rewards, and proactive surt.
  • Promote longterm contracts (12 years) with discounts or bundled offers to lock in cusers.
  • Encourage automatic payments by offering incentives (e.g., cashback, fee waivers) to reduce churisk.
  • Investigate Fiber optic service issues improve reliability, pricing, or customer support to address hichurn.
  • Segment highcharge customers and offer personalized retention strategies (discounts, premiumpport).
  • Monitor churn drivers continuously with dashboards and predictive models to act before customers leave.
